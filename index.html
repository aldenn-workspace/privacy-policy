What This Is

Aldenn is a personal AI assistant built and operated by Mike Collett for private, individual use. It connects to third-party services (like Whoop) solely to retrieve personal data for the account owner.

Data We Collect

When you authorize Aldenn to connect to a service, we access only the data scopes you explicitly approve. This may include:

  • Health and fitness metrics (sleep, recovery, strain, heart rate)
  • Profile information necessary for authentication

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Generate personal daily briefings and reports
  • Store locally on your own hardware for your reference

That's it. No analytics. No advertising. No sharing.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on private hardware controlled by the account owner. No data is transmitted to cloud servers, third parties, or external databases.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or distribute your data to anyone. Period.

Data Retention

Data is retained locally for as long as you find it useful. You can delete it at any time by removing the local files.

Your Rights

You can revoke Aldenn's access to any connected service at any time through that service's account settings. Upon revocation, no further data will be collected.
